Erin Besseau

Erin Besseau is a multimedia visual artist focusing on transient forms and motifs.

Besseau heralds from a small town outside of Ottawa Ontario and uses experiences growing up in the countryside to create imagery that discusses transformation, nostalgia and growth.

Focusing heavily on the way mental health impacts her day to day life, Besseau uses her art to create a feeling of safety and catharsis that is amplified by the soft blue tones in her most recent body of work. Besseau is a 2017 alumnus of the Nova Scotia College of Art and Design University.
